Places Must Visit

Yellowstone National Park

I visited in June 2017.

Yellowstone Nationl Park is a beautiful place. There is lots of wildlife and a beautiful view. When I went to Yellowstone National Park I saw flaming hot gysers and rainbows too. Me and my family also stopped to take a quick swim in a little river. The water was very clean and it was so fun! There were many waterfalles in Yellowstone, some HUGE some small. There was one waterfall names the Hidden waterfall because it is hard to see as it is hidden behine mane trees.Yellowstone is home to many animals. I saw a foxes, bisons, bears, etc. Yellowstone can also be a verycozy place. I enjoyed Yellowstone immensly. You should also go to visit sometime.
